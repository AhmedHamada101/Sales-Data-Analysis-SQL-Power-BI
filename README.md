**Purpose:** 
- Analyze sales data to identify trends, top-selling products, and revenue metrics for business decision-making. 


**Description:** 
- In this project, we will dive into a large sales dataset to extract valuable insights. we will explore sales trends over time, identify the best-selling products, calculate revenue metrics such as total sales and profit margins, and create visualizations to present your findings effectively. This project showcases your  ability to analyze and derive insights from large datasets, enabling you to make data-driven recommendations to optimize sales strategies.


**Steps⬇:**
- Used SQL for data cleaning and Transformation (handling missing values, formatting, and data consistency) to ensure reliable insights. 
- Used SQL to analyze data and answer questions (Exploratory Data Analysis).
- Analyze sales data to identify trends, top-selling products, and revenue metrics for business decision-making.
- Developed an interactive and dynamic Dashboard using Pivot Tables, Slicers, and advanced charting techniques to track Sales Performance.
